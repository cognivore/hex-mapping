;on names
1,<p>**Names:** When Chinese or Japanese names are being used, the order is *surname* then *given name*; other names use the order *given name* then *surname*; some people have no surname.

;on stats
1,<p>**Stats:** Characteristics are given for a 2d6 system with values ranging from 2 to 12, using A, B, and C for 10, 11, and 12. For a person, the *Universal Personality Profile* (UPP) consists of strength, dexterity, endurance, intelligence, education, and social standing. For a system, the *Universal World Profile* (UWP) consists of starport (A-E, F), size, atmosphere, water, population, government, law, a dash, and tech level.

;on equipment
1,<p>**Equipment:** It is assumed that every non-player character in this document comes equipped with at least one weapon they are skilled in. Remember that a ship’s locker contains the basics for survival on an alien word, plus equipment to defend against boarding: protective clothing, vacc suits, tools, portable shelters, but also weapons such as shotguns, carbines, pistols, and ammunition.
